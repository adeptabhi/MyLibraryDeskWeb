My Library Desk — Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy explains exactly what information the My Library Desk mobile application collects, stores, and processes.

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Library Desk mobile application ("App") developed for library management. By registering or using the App, the library administrator and its members agree to the collection, storage, and processing of information as described below. We only collect data that is strictly required to operate, optimize, and manage the daily activities of the library. If you do not agree with any terms of this policy, please do not download, install, or register with the App.

2 How We Use Your Information

All collected data is processed and used exclusively to facilitate, optimize, and maintain smooth operations within the library management system. We do not use this data for profiling or advertising. Specifically, we use the information to:

  • Register and manage the library, its administrator account, and settings
  • Add, update, and manage member profiles with identity verification
  • Create and configure library shifts with pricing and timings
  • Assign seats and lockers to members and track availability
  • Record, display, and manage payment and subscription transactions
  • Track member active/inactive status and enrollment history
  • Generate internal reports for the library administrator
  • Send payment due reminders and membership expiry alerts

Processing these records helps ensure transparency in financial transactions, prevent locker booking conflicts, and manage seat configurations in real-time.

3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is hosted and stored on secure cloud database servers. We enforce strict administrative and technical measures designed to protect database records from loss, theft, alteration, disclosure, or unauthorized access in transit and at rest.

🔒 Encrypted Passwords
🛡️ Secure API (HTTPS)
🔑 Token-Based Auth
📦 Server-Side Validation
Data Retention & Deletion: All member profiles, transaction logs, seat allocations, and shift records are preserved only for the duration of your active library registration. Library administrators can request complete, irreversible database erasure at any time, which we will execute within standard operational timelines.

4 Sharing of Information

We value your trust and maintain strict data boundaries. We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal database profiles, contact records, or transaction histories with third-party advertising companies or target marketing platforms. Access to your information is strictly limited to the following scenarios:

  • By the authorized library administrator through the App
  • When required by applicable law or a valid court order
  • To prevent fraud or protect the security of the App

5 Your Rights

We believe in giving you complete control over your personal records. Depending on your local jurisdiction and system role, you hold the following rights regarding the database profiles associated with your account:

📋 View your data
✏️ Update information
🗑️ Request deletion
📤 Export records
🚫 Withdraw consent

To submit a request regarding these rights, library members should contact their respective library administrator directly. If you are an administrator, you may email us directly using the contact methods below, and we will process your query within standard verification timelines.

6 Children's Privacy

My Library Desk is designed and intended for use by students, library visitors, and workspace subscribers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not intentionally or knowingly collect, store, or solicit personal information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has submitted personal details to the App, please reach out to us immediately, and we will take swift action to purge such records from our databases.
